The Space

A creative studio in the heart of Visby — set among chandeliers, vintage jackets and instruments lining every wall. Built to capture the moment, not to impress with square meters.

The Ludwig 1963 kit and Roland TD-50 electronic drums are always ready. Guitars hang on the walls, the Allen & Heath console runs straight into Logic Pro through UAD plugins. Everything you need to record, rehearse or just hang out and create.
