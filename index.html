About

Instrumentalist, drummer and composer based on the island of Gotland, Sweden. Recording from a boutique studio in Visby — writing, arranging and playing drums, percussion, bass, guitars and piano.

The sound moves between desert blues, world music and Nordic folk — earthy, grooving and warm. Every release is a new session, a new constellation.
